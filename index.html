Nintendo is a company that everybody at least somewhat knows about. Even less are aware what fans and third parties have done with them. This website aims to change that. From the Nintendo Entertainment System (NES) to the Wii to the Switch and Switch 2, This is a resource aimed to showcase a history of Nindo's consoles and their Homebrew history.
